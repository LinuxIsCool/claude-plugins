Hi Shawn

A quick update from Deya and I.

For the last 6 weeks we have been on a deep dive recording
pilgrimage throughout Egypt - ancient Kemet!

4 days after the completion of the Australian tour we jumped on a
plane and flew straight to Egypt.

It has been a 13 year wait to get a YES for this journey into
Planetary Grid UVG Point 1 - and it has been nothing less than
incredible!

Deya has deep listened and sang with incredible ancient stones,
temples and ancestors. From the Western Sahara desert to the
Great Pyramids & Sphinx of Giza, to the Goddess temples and
sacred waters of the Nile and beyond.

Songs of deep connection, activation, awakening, honouring and
celebration...

I have filmed and documented the journey.

We both have wept so many tears of remembrance and joy!

All we can say right now is Wow! Wow! Wow! as Deya and I continue
to integrate the experience.

We feel so grateful and blessed to have had this opportunity.

And now Deya is excited to be in the studio to begin collating &
journeying with the songs to get them ready to share with you.

Big Love
Hamilton & Deya
