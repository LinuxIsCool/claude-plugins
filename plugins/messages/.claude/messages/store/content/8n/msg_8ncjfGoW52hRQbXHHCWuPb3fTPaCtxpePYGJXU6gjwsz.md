How we improved your workflow this year
---------------------------------------

This year we focused on improvements that made your workflow faster and easier to use.

Flexible portfolio strategies

* Support for long and short positions in Model Portfolios

A clearer structure for client relationships

* Our new three tier structure lets you group Accounts into Portfolios and Portfolios into Households, giving you a clear, organized view of a client’s full financial picture

Faster ways to bring client data into Koyfin

* PDF statement uploads that turn files into working portfolios ready for proposals
* New custodian & PMS integrations including Altruist, Black Diamond, IBKR, Orion, and TradePMR, with Fidelity and Addepar coming soon

More customizable proposals

* Custom pages for introducing your team, process, and investment approach
* Adjustable portfolio values, returns, fees, and sensitivity inputs to clearly show the effect of your recommendations

Clearer comparisons in client reports

* New comparison pages that highlight differences in fees, allocations, exposures, holdings, instrument types, and more

More updates released this year

* Higher portfolio capacity: Support up to 2k holdings in Client Portfolios and 1k in Model Portfolios
* Custom data series: Create your own data series for private funds, real estate, or other external assets, and use them in charts, watchlists, and portfolios
* One-pager reports: Create clean, single-page client summaries for quick updates
* Desktop alerts: Get notified about price moves, news, filings, and portfolio changes
* Transcript summaries: Read structured summaries of earnings and other company calls
* Portfolio Views: Save custom column and layout configurations in Client Portfolios and reuse them across accounts and teams
* SMA data: Research and add US SMA strategies alongside other holdings in portfolios
