Hey there,

We're reaching out to let you know that effective January 1, 2026, pursuant to
effective regulatory filings, the following exchanges will modify their Options
Regulatory Fee (ORF), as shown below.

What's Changing?

The MIAX Exchange Group is changing their ORFs as follows:
 * $0.0017 per contract on MIAX Options Exchange
 * $0.0016 per contract on MIAX Pearl Options Exchange

Read MIAX Exchange Group's announcement
[https://www.miaxglobal.com/alert/2025/12/01/miax-exchange-members-options-regulatory-fee]
for more information.

Cboe is changing their ORF as follows:
 * $0.0023 per contract on Cboe Options
 * $0.0002 per contract on BZX Options
 * $0.0002 per contract on EDGX Options
 * $0.0003 per contract on C2 Options

Read Cboe's full announcement
[https://cdn.cboe.com/resources/fee_schedule/2026/Cboe-Options-Exchanges-Regulatory-Fee-Update-Effective-January-2-2026.pdf?_gl=1*1cu81ig*_up*MQ..*_ga*MTc5MDU4MzEyLjE3NjQ2OTc1NTk.*_ga_5Q99WB9X71*czE3NjQ2OTc1NTYkbzEkZzAkdDE3NjQ2OTc1NTYkajYwJGwwJGgzNDIxOTU4MzU.]
for more information.

The NYSE is changing their ORFs as follows:
 * $0.0026 per contract on NYSE AMEX Options
 * $0.0026 per contract on NYSE ARCA Options


What this means for you:

We are updating our Option Regulatory Fees (ORF) based on the latest exchange
rate schedules. There is no action required and no change on your end. These
updates will be applied automatically and will go into effect on January 1,
2026.

If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to our support team at
support@alpaca.markets [support@alpaca.markets].
