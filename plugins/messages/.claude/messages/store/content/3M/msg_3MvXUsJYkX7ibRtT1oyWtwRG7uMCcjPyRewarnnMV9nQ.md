Shawn,

Thank you to everyone who participated in our 2025 Annual General Meeting &mdash; whether you voted, volunteered, or helped shape the conversations that will guide our movement into the year ahead.

This AGM reflected the strength and clarity of our shared values. Here&rsquo;s a snapshot of the key results from the weekend.
&#128499; Provincial Council Elections
Members elected the following candidates to Provincial Council:

	- 
Vice Chair: Farrukh Chishtie &mdash; 70.8%

	- 
Secretary: Marie Ward &mdash; 94.4%

	- 
Ombudsperson: Kathy Porter &mdash; 74.3%

	- 
Councillor-at-Large: Prym Goodacre &mdash; 55.1%

	- 
Regional Councillor, Okanagan&ndash;Kootenays: Bradley Bartsch &mdash; 89.8%

	- 
Regional Councillor, Lower Mainland: Nicola Spurling &mdash; 51.9%

	- 
Regional Councillor, Vancouver Island & Sunshine Coast: Severin Odland &mdash; 77.1%

We&rsquo;re grateful to every candidate who put their name forward. To all members who voted, thank you!
&#128196; Motions Passed
Your votes set our direction for the coming year. Key motions approved include:

	- 
Standing firmly against all forms of oppression, including apartheid, systemic discrimination, genocide, and colonization &mdash; 94.8%

	- 
Approving the 2024 financial report &mdash; 98.7%

	- 
Approving the auditor&rsquo;s report & reappointing The Accounting Agency as auditor &mdash; 99.2% and 98.9% respectively

	- 
Confirming no leadership contest before the 2026 AGM &mdash; 96.8%

	- 
Approving Version 2 of the BC Greens&rsquo; policy document (&ldquo;Shared Future: A Green Plan for Wellbeing&rdquo;) &mdash; 69.1%

	- 
Forming a member negotiation committee & requiring member ratification of agreements with other parties &mdash; 74.6%

One motion, to explore moving to a co-leadership model, did not pass (48.9% in favour).
&#128154; Thank you
This AGM wasn&rsquo;t just procedural. It was a powerful expression of who we are as a party. Members showed up with clarity, commitment, and a shared vision for a more just, compassionate, and sustainable province.

Your participation keeps this movement democratic, member-driven, and grounded in our values. Thank you for being part of this work.

Onward,

Your BC Greens team
