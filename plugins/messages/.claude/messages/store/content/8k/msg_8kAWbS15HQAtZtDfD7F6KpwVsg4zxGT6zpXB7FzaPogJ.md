   - We will try: [describe what you will try to build evidence about your
   hunch/guess/assumption]
   - Our confidence will increase when: [describe the qualitative and/or
   quantitative evidence that will increase your confidence in your
   hunch/guess/assumption]
   - Our confidence will decrease when: [describe the qualitative and/or
   quantitative evidence that will decrease your confidence in your
   hunch/guess/assumption]
   - We expect to have sufficient evidence in: [the timeframe you expect to
   have enough evidence to evaluate this belief]

   increased confidence what you would try next]
