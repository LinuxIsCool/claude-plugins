# **Now back to Loopini!**


--------------------
View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/16466d27-5495-4e66-bad5-9725ec50d508/8.png?t=1763340609)

Driven by a growing national focus on weight management and nutrition (exacerbated by the rise of GLP-1 drugs and increasing health awareness), consumers are actively seeking fast, low-commitment, healthy meal options. **The U.S. frozen pizza market alone was valued at $6.62 billion in 2022** and is expected to grow significantly.

Loopini has a unique value proposition within this market because it is at the intersection of convenience and health. Perfect for our age of instant gratification, Loopini bypasses the extensive time and preparation typically associated with healthy food, offering a compelling solution for the modern, health-focused individual.

Their secret? **The Lupini bean.** This Italian superfood is the primary ingredient in their dough, thriving in drought-prone conditions and requiring 88% less water than traditional ingredients. By partnering with a local Italian manufacturer, Loopini has created a supply chain that can‚Äôt be replicated. It is also one that is climate-friendly, which could result in an even stronger positioning proposition for the brand moving forward, as 50% of U.S. consumers have stated they are willing to pay more for climate-friendly food options.

View image: (https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/7ae387a4-f087-4442-87d9-82b7b059723c/9.png?t=1763340628)

Loopini has several channels designed to maximize their sales footprint. The company started by piloting in New York City and the Midwest to develop product-market-fit and a correct pricing strategy. Today, **Loopini has sold 14,000+ pies** and boasts some remarkable statistics about how their brand has been a game-changer for the frozen pizza category: 60% of their customers did not buy frozen pizza before Loopini, and they are currently selling three times faster than competitor brands in the storefronts that they are currently available in.

In 2026, Loopini will conduct a regional expansion into key retail locations, focusing on growth within **Whole Foods, Sprouts Farmers Market, and Target**. The company has product requests from **7,000 retail locations** and is currently building up its supply chain‚Äôs capabilities to meet this demand. Within their first three months of sales in grocery storefronts, Loopini has reduced their cost of goods sold by 37.5% and enabled a mass production scale increase of 20 times their initial capacity. Based off their internal estimates, the company is on track for profitability by 2027.

Loopini‚Äôs product is a differentiated offering in the saturated frozen pizza market. The high-protein, high-fiber formulation, combined with an authentic Italian taste, gives Loopini a significant edge over competitors like Against the Grain and Banza Pizza, which fall short when compared to Loopini's superior macros. This differentiation is critical in a space where consumer switching costs are negligible.

Loopini's unique positioning and strong growth in the functional food space make it an attractive acquisition target for large food conglomerates. The frozen pizza category offers clear exit viability, one that has existed since Nestl√©‚Äôs $3.7B acquisition of Kraft‚Äôs frozen pizza business in 2010.

To win, they will have to demonstrate why they offer a pizza that is worth the investment, highlighting everything from Italian ingredients & production to superior health macros. 

Despite this challenge, Loopini's premium positioning, strong early traction, and scalable manufacturing foundation provide a promising path toward national expansion and long-term profitability. After all: if Whole Foods & Target see it, then why shouldn‚Äôt we?
