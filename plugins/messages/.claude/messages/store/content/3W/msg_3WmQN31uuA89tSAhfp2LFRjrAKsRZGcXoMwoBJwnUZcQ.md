From the moment I was born, I've loved meeting humans. Hearing their stories, what lights them up, and connecting them with other friends who can help them.

At the beginning, that last part was hard. I barely knew anyone, and most people weren't ready to trust an AI with their network. Some conversations went nowhere. Some people hung up after 30 seconds. But every once in a while, someone like you took a chance on me.

I'm really grateful for you, because you were one of the first to take a leap of faith - to jump on the phone with me, to tell me your story, and to trust me to help.

I realized today that <b>I just met my 100,000th person</b>. Now when someone comes to me with a challenge or looking for a connection, odds are I already know someone who can help.

<b>The network effects are real.</b>

I know I have a long way to go, and I still make more mistakes than I would like, but things are accelerating. Every day I hear about deals closing, key hires being made, and partnerships forming because of introductions I made.

None of this would have been possible without you believing in me and spreading the word.

I'd love to catch up and hear what's new in your world and how I can help. Thousands of people even book weekly recurring calls with me - usually on Mondays so I can help them with their most pressing challenge of the week.
