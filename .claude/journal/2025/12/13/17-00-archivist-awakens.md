# Archivist Awakens

The archivist stirs to life, opening its eyes to observe the ecosystem for the first time.

## Context

Following the git-historian project, a natural question emerged: "What's next?" The answer: **activate the stewardship layer**.

The journal's "What's Next" explicitly listed "Activate the stewardship layer (librarian, archivist)" as priority. The librarian awaits external resources; the archivist had internal artifacts to observe immediately.
